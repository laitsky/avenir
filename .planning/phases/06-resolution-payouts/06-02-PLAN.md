<objective>
Implement the compute_payouts MPC infrastructure: comp_def registration, queue instruction, and callback handler.

Purpose: This is the MPC bridge between resolve_market (Plan 01) and claim_payout (Plan 03). After the creator resolves a market, anyone calls compute_payouts to trigger the MPC circuit that decrypts pool totals. The callback writes the revealed values to Market and sets state=Finalized(4), enabling payout claims.

Output: Three new MPC instruction files, updated mpc/mod.rs and lib.rs entry points, updated test helpers.
</objective>
