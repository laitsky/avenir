<rationale>
The CONTEXT.md locked decision states: "Creator can resolve anytime after resolution_time (deadline). No upper time limit in Phase 6 -- the 48h grace period and dispute escalation are Phase 8's concern."

RES-02 says: "Resolution has a 48-hour grace period after market deadline." This means both:
1. Creator can resolve AFTER the deadline (implemented in Phase 6 via `require!(clock >= resolution_time)`)
2. Creator cannot resolve AFTER the 48h window closes (NOT implemented -- deferred to Phase 8 per CONTEXT.md)

Since the full requirement is not satisfied by Phase 6's implementation, RES-02 must be reclassified to Phase 8 where the upper bound will be enforced alongside dispute escalation (which triggers when the grace period expires without resolution).

Implementing the upper bound in Phase 6 would VIOLATE the user's locked decision and could cause issues: if Phase 8 is not yet built, markets where the creator misses the 48h window would be permanently unresolvable (no dispute system to fall back to).
</rationale>
</context>
